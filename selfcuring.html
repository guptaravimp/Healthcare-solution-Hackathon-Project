Fever

Symptoms

A fever is defined as a body temperature of 100.4°F (38°C) or higher. Fever is often a sign that the body is fighting off an infection or illness

Precautions:-
Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of fluids, such as water, clear broths, and electrolyte solutions, to prevent dehydration and help regulate body temperature. Rest: Give your body the rest it needs to recover. Avoid strenuous activities when you have a fever.

Medicine:-bAcetaminophen (Tylenol): Acetaminophen is a fever reducer and pain reliever. It's a widely used medication to lower fever and alleviate discomfort.
