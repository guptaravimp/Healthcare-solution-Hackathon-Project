Public Private partnership

Public-private partnerships (PPPs) are collaborative arrangements between government entities and private sector organizations to jointly fund, develop, and operate projects or services that typically fall under the purview of the public sector. These partnerships can take various forms and are often used to leverage the expertise, resources, and efficiency of both sectors to deliver public goods and services. Collaborations between public health departments and private technology companies can enhance health information systems, making patient data more accessible, secure, and interoperable. Private sector partnerships can provide state-of-the-art medical equipment, technology, and software solutions to public healthcare facilities in underserved areas. Public-private partnerships can establish and operate telehealth platforms, enabling patients in remote areas to access healthcare services remotely.

Our Counsellors

A healthcare counselor is a professional who plays a crucial role in helping individuals and families navigate the complex healthcare system, make informed healthcare decisions, and cope with medical conditions and treatment options. Patient Advocacy: Healthcare counselors act as advocates for patients, ensuring that their rights and preferences are respected and that they receive appropriate care. Patient Education: They provide patients with information about their medical conditions, treatment options, and available resources, helping them make informed decisions. Emotional Support: Healthcare counselors offer emotional support and coping strategies to individuals and families dealing with medical conditions, chronic illnesses, or disabilities. Care Coordination: They assist in coordinating various aspects of a patient's care, including appointments, referrals to specialists, and follow-up care. Healthcare Navigation: Healthcare counselors help patients understand health insurance, medical billing, and financial assistance programs to reduce the financial burden of healthcare.
